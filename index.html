Hannah Bridges

Hannah is a native of Western North Carolina and is deeply passionate about the people and the mountains she calls home. Hannah was led into the world of advocating for children and families with special needs by her two children who both have unique challenges. She has worked with teens and adults on the Autism Spectrum to increase independence skills in their homes and communities and coordinated support groups throughout WNC for the Autism Society of NC. Hannah is very excited to be able to further support the WNC community as a Parent Educator with ECAC!

Hannah is currently continuing her education in Data Analytics with the hopes of using new these skills to further her advocacy and nonprofit work!
